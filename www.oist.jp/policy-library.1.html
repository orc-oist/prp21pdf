Policy Library

Principles and processes for getting things done at the OIST Graduate University are set out in chapter form in this Library of Policies, Rules & Procedures. Core values, Code of Conduct, and other founding principles of the University are found in Chapter 1, Who We Are.  The University's organizational structure, executive roles and authority, legal name and additional similar information is laid out in Chapter 2, Governance & Organization.

Click on any title below to be taken to its internal Table of Contents.  You may also search the entire Library using the Search Box in the dashboard.
